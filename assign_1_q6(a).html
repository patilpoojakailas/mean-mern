Navi Mumbai Sports Association (NMSA) is a Non-Government Sports Organisation registered with Charity Commissioner under BPT Act F/737/THANE and Societies Registration Act MAH/815/THANE. It is governed by an elected body for promotion of sports, fellowship and fitness for individual, families, schools, institutions and corporate bodies.
